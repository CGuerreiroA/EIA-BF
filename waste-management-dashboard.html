Our Mission & Approach
Human life is at the heart of our mission, and delivering life-saving assistance remains our absolute priority. To ensure this aid truly supports people's well-being now and into the future, we embrace a sustainable approach. This includes utilizing voucher assistance to empower communities, implementing responsible waste management practices, and rehabilitating essential water points. These vital actions are fundamental to protecting the environment that directly sustains human life, health, and dignity.

Construction Waste Management Protocol
Current Best Practices
  • Tiles and bricks being repurposed
  • Some metal component recovery
  • Local material sourcing when possible

  • Zero formal reuse/recycling measures
  • No systematic debris sorting

Recommended Actions
  • Mandatory waste sorting protocols
  • Material recovery targets (80% reuse)
  • Rainwater harvesting during construction
